    Directions From 10 Freeway

    Take the 10 Freeway and exit on Barranca Avenue. Head north on Barranca
    toward the mountains for about 2.5 miles and turn left on Covina Blvd. The
    church is on Covina Blvd. between Barranca Avenue and Citrus Avenue.

    Directions From 210 Freeway

    Take the 210 Freeway and exit on Citrus Avenue. Head south on Citrus
    Avenue away from the mountains for about 1.5 miles and turn left on
    Covina Blvd. The church is on Covina Blvd. between Barranca Avenue
    and Citrus Avenue.

    Directions From 57 Freeway

    Take the 57 Freeway and exit on Covina Blvd. Head west on Covina
    Blvd. for about 3.5 miles. The church is on Covina Blvd. between Barranca
    Avenue and Citrus Avenue.
