    Welcome from the Pastor


    About Us

    Dear Visitor,

    New Heart Foursquare Church has a desire to minister to each individual as a whole person and to minister to each family in the same manner. Our church ministries our designed to accomplish this goal because we believe that God has a special purpose for every single person. As a result, we think you will find a personal touch at our church that you will not find elsewhere. Jesus is also at the forefront of our ministry. We strive every day to treat others better than ourselves and to foster a loving and serving community that models His ministry. We believe that there is no greater gift than having a friend like Jesus who would lay his life down on the cross for us. Thank you for visiting New Heart Foursquare Church's website! We hope to see you and your family at some point in the near future.

    Love in Christ,
    Pastor Chuck & Marianne Cannizzaro
