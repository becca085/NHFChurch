    Our Vision

    Reaching People's Hearts with the Love of Christ

    Our Mission

    Starting friendships with others through fellowship
    and active engagement. Adopting people into the family
    of God through love, service, and sacrifice. Valuing
    God's call on people's lives through spiritual mentoring
    and discipleship. Empowering people with the Holy
    Spirit to reach others for Christ.
